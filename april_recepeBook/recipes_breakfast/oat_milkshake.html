Fruit & Oat Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana.

  • 1 tbsp porridge oats.

  • 80g soft fruits (i.e. strawberries, blueberries, mango).

  • 150 ml milk.

  • 1 tsp honey.

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract.

Preparation:


STEP 1:

Put all the ingredients in a blender and whiz for 1 minute until smooth.

STEP 2:

Pour the milkshake into two glasses to serve.
